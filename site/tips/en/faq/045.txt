Display videos on the return screen
--------

For performance reasons, videos are played by default only on the public screen.
To display videos on other screens, manually enable the settings according to the display type below.

<b>Videos played via VLC Player</b>
- Click the "three dots" icon on the VLC mini player in the main window
- Menu "View (Video)"
