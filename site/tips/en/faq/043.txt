[Linux] Error searching songs on the internet
--------
<b>javax.net.ssl.SSLHandshakeException:
sun.security.validator.ValidatorException: No trusted certificate found</b>

Download the file <a href='https://www.holyrics.com.br/download/file/cacerts'>cacerts</a>
And paste in the folder:
<code>/opt/Holyrics/Holyrics/jre/lib/security/</code>

Open or restart the program and test
